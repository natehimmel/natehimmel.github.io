WE ARE HIRING FOR FALL 2026

The lab is recruiting team members intersted in any and all core research areas. If interested, please read below and email me (Nate).

  • Technician: The lab will be hiring a full-time technician to support our molecular biology and insect programs. An undergraduate degree in biology, chemistry, or a related disciplne is required. Experience with Drosophila melanogaster is preferred, but not required. Please check back for the official job posting in 2026, but feel free to send an email for more information.
  • Undergraduates: The lab will open to undergraduates in 2026; please check back in 2026 for details.
  • Masters: The lab will open to Master's project/thesis students in 2026; please check back in 2026 for details.
  • PhD: The lab is actively recruiting PhD students. The lab encourages and will support fellowship applications for competitive students. Please review the requirements for the PhD in Biology. The deadline for applications (to startin fall 2026) is December 15, 2025. Please feel free to send an email for more information.
  • Postdocs: The lab will support postdocs in applying for independent funding (e.g. NIH F32 or K99, HFSP Long-Term Fellowships, EMBO postdoc fellowships, and so on). The lab is open to any relevant project an aspiring postdoc wishes to lead. Please send an email expressing your interest, and include a CV.
